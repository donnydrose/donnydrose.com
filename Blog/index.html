Technically Speaking

As I continue this journey from bartending to UX and Sound Design, I want this blog to be more than just personal stories. It’s going to be a resource where you can follow along with my creative process and see how I build things from the ground up.

In the design world, I’ll be sharing my process as I build landing pages, create user experiences, and experiment with UI/UX principles.

In addition to design, music is a huge part of my creative life. Expect to find some original soundtracks I create, whether it’s for video content or just new ideas I’m experimenting with like my song journal.

It’s going to be a journey of growth, and I hope you’ll join me for it.

Reflecting With Gratitude

2024 has been a year of uncertainty, filled with both highs and lows. At times, I've found myself chasing moments and energies, seeking answers without knowing the right questions. Despite the challenges, I'm grateful for each breath, my family, and the moments of clarity I've gained. Loneliness in a city of 8 million people is real, but I've come to understand the difference between fleeting exchanges and true connection. As I look to 2025, I'm approaching the new year with intention–focusing on cleaner living, mindful practice, and keeping gratitude close to my heart. I believe these principles will help me navigate the challenges ahead and celebrate moments of personal growth.
