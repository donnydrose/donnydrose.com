Halloween 2024: Neighborhood Watch

Production and Process Notes

Here We Have a some Hideous Halloween Fun. Oh the Horror. Photo's from around my neighborhood in Brooklyn. The idea was to build a lo-fi yet fun webpage to celebrate the folks in my neighborhood who went all out for the rest of us.

  1. Take photos on a walk after work, usually 2am
  2. Use raw photo and footage, lo-fi, real theme, spooky, time management
  3. Use photo as header, get rid of links
  4. Use creepster font family from g-fonts
  5. Use orangered for color on h1 heading, and slightly more red hue for h2 heading
  6. Backup with websafe-colors
  7. Trim videos, use quick time
  8. Quick time formats to .mov file (not great for all browsers)
  9. Found 'Handbrake' - French encoding software
  10. Encoded .mov to .mp4 - most universal browser compatibility
  11. All videos are in portrait
  12. Learn better phone photography, but this works for now
